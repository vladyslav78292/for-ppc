IPL Playoffs

As we mentioned earlier, 10 teams are competing in the IPL league. However, only 4 of them can go through to the IPL playoffs. Those are the teams that finish at the top 4 positions when the main rounds come to an end.

Now, in the IPL playoffs schedule, the first two teams will play against each other in the quarter-finals. The teams ranked 3rd and 4th will also meet each other in the other quarter-final match.

Here, the winner from the first quarter-final (1st vs 2nd) will go directly to the final. However, the loser will play in the semi-final against the winner from the other quarter-final match (3rd vs 4th). In the end, the winner will get to play in the final against the team that won the first quarter-final.

Gujarat Titans

The Gujarat Titans are a team from Ahmedabad, Gujarat that began competing in 2022. Despite its lack of experience, this young team managed to win the IPL title in the 2022 season. They are also the favourite to win the IPL 2024, but that is just according to the bookmakers.

Last season, the Gujarat Titans were 1st in the league but lost to Chennai Super Kings in both the quarter-finals and the final. It's fair to say that they have one of the best squads in the whole IPL.

Chennai Super Kings

Next, we have the Chennai Super Kings, a team from Chennai, Tamil Nadu that has a long history in the IPL. They feature 5 IPL titles, the same as the Mumbai Indians, making them the teams with the most titles.

The Chennai Super Kings are also the current IPL champion, after winning the 2023 season against the Gujarat Titans. They hold the record for appearing in the most finals (10) and for the most playoff qualifications (12). This team also features two titles in the CLT20.

It is worth noting that this team didn't play in the IPL in seasons 2016 and 2017, due to suspension. This was due to their owners' involvement in the 2013 betting case.

Mumbai Indians

Mumbai Indians is another legendary team that has been competing in the IPL since 2008. They play their home matches in Mumbai, Maharashtra and they feature 5 IPL titles, just like the Chennai Super Kings.

Last season, they lost to the Gujarat Titans in the semi-finals, after beating Lucknow Super Giants in the quarter-finals. Their last title in the IPL was in 2020 and they are hungrier than ever for a new success. It is also worth noting that the Mumbai Indians feature two titles in the CLT20.

Rajasthan Royals

The Rajasthan Royals are also one of the teams that are playing in the IPL right from the beginning. Since its establishment in 2008, this team managed to win the league once. That was actually the first year of the IPL. After this major success, the Rajasthan Royals are struggling to repeat its glory.

However, they are still among the favourites as they feature a strong squad. Rajasthan Royals were close to winning the IPL 2022, but the Gujarat Titans were slightly better than them. Last season they failed to qualify for the playoffs after finishing at the 5th spot.

Lucknow Super Giants

Lastly, we have the Lucknow Super Giants, another young team that started competing in 2022. During their first season, they finished in the 3rd position but failed to qualify for the semi-finals.

In 2023, this team ended up in the same league position. Yet, they once again lost their quarter-final match. Many look at these seasons as failures, but they have to remember that this is still a young team. If they continue with their progress then they can surely dream of winning the IPL title.

Shubman Gill

We start off with Shubman Gill, a 24-year-old player from India who is actually the captain of the Gujarat Titans. He is one of the best batsmen in the IPL as well as in the world. During the 2023 IPL season, he managed the most runs (890), scoring the second-highest in IPL history.

Faf du Plessis

Faf du Plessis is a South African batsman that many consider to be one of the greatest fielders of all time. He's the captain of the Royal Challengers Bangalore and is still on top of his game despite being 39 years old. du Plessis is also considered to be one of the best all-format batsmen of his era.

Virat Kohli

Another great IPL betting player that you should know about is Virat Kohli. This batter has played for the Royal Challengers Bangalore since 2008, being one of its longest-serving players. Fans acknowledge him as one of the greatest batsmen in the history of cricket. He is the highest run-scorer in the IPL and T20I.

Yashasvi Jaiswal

Yashavi Jaiswal is a 21-year-old batter who plays for the Rajasthan Royals. Thanks to his great performances on the field, many believe that he has a bright future in cricket. In 2019, Jaiswal became the youngest player to score a List A cricket double-century.

Suryakumar Yadav

Lastly, we have Suryakumar Yadav, a top batter who plays for the Mumbai Indians. He is also the vice-captain of the Indian National Cricket Team, representing his country in all three formats. IPL fans know him as one of the finest batters in the modern era.
